Built-in Themes

Uplink ships with six themes, each with a distinct personality. Themes control background colors, accent colors, text colors, chat bubble styling, and the overall feel of the interface.

Midnight

Default theme. Dark background with cyan accents. The signature Uplink look.

Sakura

Pink-toned dark theme. Soft, warm, inspired by cherry blossoms.

Ember

Warm orange accents on dark. Energetic and bold.

Forest

Green-tinted dark theme. Calm, focused, terminal-inspired.

Daylight

The only light theme. Warm cream background with washed red accents. Easy on the eyes during the day.

Phantom

Dark and muted. Subtle slate tones with no flashy accents. Maximum focus.
